Zoo Bar - Hotel Ambassadeur

This project involved the transformation and enhancement of an outdoor space through a series of targeted construction interventions. A custom shade structure was installed using a robust system of metal beams, providing both durability and effective sun protection while maintaining a clean architectural aesthetic. New sanitary facilities were designed and constructed, including the installation of modern, fully functional toilets that meet current standards of comfort and hygiene. The scope of work also included the construction of an outdoor bar area, creating a welcoming and practical space that enhances user experience and supports social interaction. All works were completed in compliance with relevant safety, structural, and design requirements, ensuring both functionality and long-term reliability.
