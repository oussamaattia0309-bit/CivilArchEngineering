Villa Mutuelleville – Terrace Waterproofing & Tiling

Complete renovation of an existing terrace, including the removal of old waterproofing and surface preparation. We handled the demolition, stripping of the old membrane, and debris removal.

Application of a new waterproofing membrane to ensure full protection against moisture and water infiltration.

Installation of a mortar screed for proper leveling and surface preparation.

Finishing with high-quality tiles, laid with professional-grade adhesive for a durable, long-lasting result.
