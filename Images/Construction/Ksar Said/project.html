Villa Ksar Said – Complete Interior Renovation & Finishing Works

We provide full-scope interior renovation services for residential and commercial spaces. Our team manages the entire process, from initial demolition to the final high-quality finish. We specialize in complete bathroom and room overhauls, transforming outdated layouts into modern, functional spaces. Our tile installation (carrelage) covers floors, walls, and cladding with precision using ceramic, stone, and porcelain. Our custom joinery (menuiserie) includes interior doors, built-in cabinets, shelving, and decorative moldings. We handle all plumbing work (plomberie), from fixture installation to full piping upgrades. Every project is managed for a seamless blend of durability, aesthetics, and code compliance. We ensure reliable execution with attention to detail across every trade.
